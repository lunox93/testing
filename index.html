The Solution

GakkoMonkezwill build “The Moving Picture”, an NFT marketplace for video based content. The content can range from GIFs, video clips, all the way to short films, movies and VR experiences. JPEG NFTs have changed the lives of millions of graphic designers, illustrators, animators, painters, photographers, etc.. GakkoMonkezwants to maximize this spectrum.
